# Overview

## Raw-Denoising-Project
Training of [SCUNet](https://github.com/cszn/SCUNet) for 4-channel RAW image denoising and comparison to [DnCNN](https://github.com/cszn/DnCNN).^
The Project went from May to August 2024.
